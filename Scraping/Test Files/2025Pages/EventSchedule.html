Schedule and Results

Most autocrosses are held on Saturdays and Sundays except where otherwise noted. These are our entry fees: http://ccsportscarclub.org/autocross/

The daily event schedule is usually as follows. Number of participants may require schedule to be modified. Driver Info emails are sent the night before the event to update any schedule changes.

Times* Activities
7:00 am —- Site Opens
No On site —- Registration – Online only closes 3pm day before
7:30 — 9:00am —- Tech Inspection
On your own —- Walking Tour of the Course (video link in email)
9:00-9:20am* —- Drivers Meeting, mandatory safety briefing
9:30 am* —- First Car Off
before 4:00pm —- Trophies will commence after last car’s run and results are verified

* key times subject to change

About the Auspuff

Auspuff is CCSCC's newsletter. It contains all autocross and rally event reports and results. Editorials and reports on the club are also written by executive board members.
